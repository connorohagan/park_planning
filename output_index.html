Preset map outputs

  • balanced — General-purpose trade-off: demand-led, equity-aware, avoids risky/small sites.
  • demand_focused — Maximise overall benefit to residents (total demand dominates).
  • gap_coverage — Close green-space gaps: underserved demand dominates, then park-distance.
  • accessibility_focused — Prioritise reducing distance to nearest park (network accessibility).
  • safety_first — Minimise flood exposure: flood risk dominates selection.
  • feasibility_first — Favour sites that are easier to deliver: size + flood dominate.
